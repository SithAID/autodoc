Our Purpose

THE MISSION BEHIND AUTODOC

We believe every driver deserves access to professional-grade diagnostics — without the jargon, the waiting rooms, or the unnecessary repair bills. AutoDoc makes that possible.

🎯
DEMOCRATISE DIAGNOSTICS
Professional-grade vehicle analysis should not be locked behind expensive tools and dealership visits. AutoDoc puts a master technician in your pocket.
🛡️
SAFETY ABOVE ALL
Safety-critical faults are never understated. AutoDoc is engineered to escalate brake failures, fuel leaks, and steering loss with immediate, clear warnings.
🌍
LANGUAGE INCLUSIVE
Automotive knowledge should cross language barriers. AutoDoc supports English, Spanish, French, and Sinhala — with more languages on the 2026 roadmap.
⚡
AI-FIRST APPROACH
Rather than a static fault code database, AutoDoc uses live AI reasoning. This means contextual diagnosis — not just code lookups — that improves with every interaction.

Roadmap

WHERE WE'RE GOING

AutoDoc is actively developed. Here's what's been shipped and what's coming next.

Q4 2024
AUTODOC V1 LAUNCHED
Core diagnostic chat with ChatGPT and Gemini integration. Three-language support. Basic vehicle garage.
Q1 2025
GROK & CLAUDE ADDED
Four-model support with auto-switching on quota exhaustion. DTC fault code panel. Session history autosave.
Q2 2025
WEB FRONTEND v2 SHIPPED
Full five-page web app: Home, Dashboard, Blog, About, and Diagnostic Chat. Toast system. Vehicle management.
Q4 2025
SINHALA LANGUAGE SUPPORT
Fourth language added — full diagnostic system prompt in Sinhala. Gemini auto-fallback model switching.
Q2 2026
LIVE OBD-II INTEGRATION
Bluetooth OBD-II adapter support. Real-time sensor data fed directly into AI diagnostic context.
Q4 2026
MOBILE APP & ACCOUNTS
React Native iOS and Android app. Persistent accounts with cloud-synced vehicle history and diagnosis archive.
